About
I like to make things.
I am the Lead of the Data Automation Lab in the Development Economics Debt Statistics group at the World Bank. I enjoy blending data science and design to create products that bring insight and delight.

Before the World Bank, I worked at the Grameen Foundation, the Harvard Humanitarian Initiative, and in management consulting at HWC. I received my bachelor's degree in government from Harvard University and my master's degree in international economics from UC San Diego.

This space serves as an archive of my projects. Some of them are work-related but the majority are things I made for fun.
