About
I enjoy making things, whether it's using code, data, markers, paper, etc. This is an archive of things I've made - some work projects, but mostly stuff made for fun.

I work at World Bank in the Development Economics Data Group. I'm an analyst on the Debt Statistics team, which means I get to work with financial institutions around the world to compile and analyze financial data. I also improve the usability and understanding of our data as the lead of our Data Automation Lab and creating and maintaining our debt-data GitHub repo.

I'm also a freelance illustrator and an Apple Developer, with 4 sticker apps in the App Store. One of which was ranked #1 in the Lifestyle section!

Before the World Bank, I worked at the Grameen Foundation, the Harvard Humanitarian Initiative, Plan International, and in management consulting. I received my bachelor's degree in government from Harvard University and my master's degree in international economics from UC San Diego.
